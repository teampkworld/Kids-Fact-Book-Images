## KDP Cover Creator – Preserve Original Image Quality Without Any Loss

### Required Implementation

In the **KDP Cover Creator**, every image uploaded by the user must retain its **original quality** throughout the entire cover creation and PDF export process.

The final **print-ready KDP cover PDF** must match the quality of the original uploaded images. At no stage should the application reduce image quality or apply any form of compression that affects the visual result.

### Image Quality Requirements

* Preserve the original image resolution.
* Preserve original sharpness and fine details.
* Preserve original colors, brightness, contrast, and saturation.
* Maintain the original aspect ratio without distortion.
* Keep text, logos, and illustrations crisp and clear.
* Do not introduce blur, pixelation, noise, artifacts, banding, or compression marks.
* Do not automatically resize or downscale images unless required by the selected cover dimensions.

### PDF Export Requirements

The exported KDP cover PDF must:

* Use lossless or maximum-quality image embedding.
* Preserve the full resolution of every uploaded image.
* Avoid unnecessary image recompression.
* Maintain sharp vector elements and text.
* Produce a professional, print-ready PDF suitable for Amazon KDP.

### Rendering Pipeline

Ensure that the rendering pipeline never performs destructive operations such as:

* Low-quality JPEG compression.
* Automatic image downscaling.
* Canvas rendering at reduced resolution.
* Thumbnail images being used in the final export.
* Multiple image re-encoding passes.

Always use the original uploaded image (or the highest-resolution source available) when generating the final PDF.

### Validation Checklist

Before exporting the cover PDF, verify that:

* ✅ Every uploaded image is rendered using its original quality.
* ✅ No blur, pixelation, noise, or compression artifacts are introduced.
* ✅ Colors match the original uploaded image.
* ✅ Fine details remain sharp at 100% zoom and in print.
* ✅ The exported KDP cover PDF visually matches the quality of the user's uploaded images.
* ✅ The PDF is suitable for high-quality commercial printing on Amazon KDP.

### Expected Result

The **KDP Cover Creator** must generate a **print-ready cover PDF** whose image quality is virtually identical to the user's original uploaded images, with **no visible loss of quality, blur, noise, compression artifacts, or resolution reduction**.
