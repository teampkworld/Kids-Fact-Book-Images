## Fix Book Spine Title Rendering – Preview and PDF Output

### Current Issue

After the recent implementation, the **Book Spine Title** is no longer rendering correctly.

When the **Book Spine Title Editor** is enabled and a title is entered:

* The spine title does **not** appear in the live preview.
* The spine title does **not** appear in the exported PDF.
* Only a blank spine area is displayed, even though the title and formatting options have been configured.

---

## Required Fix

Identify and resolve the rendering issue so that the spine title is displayed correctly in both the **live preview** and the **generated KDP cover PDF**.

The rendering engine must always use the current values entered in the **Book Spine Title Editor**.

---

## Rendering Logic

When **Book Spine Title Editor = Enabled**:

* Display the spine title immediately in the live preview.
* Apply all selected formatting options:

  * Font family
  * Font size
  * Bold
  * Italic
  * Underline
  * Text color
  * Background color (if enabled)
  * Letter spacing
  * Line spacing
  * Text rotation
  * Alignment
* Position the title correctly within the spine area.

When **Book Spine Title Editor = Disabled**:

* Hide the spine title completely.
* Display a blank spine.

---

## Synchronization

Ensure that the preview and PDF generation use the **same rendering logic and data source**.

There should be no separate rendering paths that can become out of sync.

Any change made in the editor must immediately update:

* Live preview
* Internal rendering state
* Final exported PDF

---

## Debug Checks

Verify that:

* The spine title value is being saved correctly.
* The rendering component receives the current title text.
* The title is not being clipped, hidden, or rendered outside the spine area.
* Font loading completes before rendering.
* Layer order (z-index/render order) does not place the title behind other elements.
* Text opacity, visibility, and color are valid.
* The spine title is included in both the preview canvas and the PDF export pipeline.

---

## Validation Checklist

Before generating the final KDP cover PDF, verify that:

* ✅ The spine title appears immediately in the live preview when enabled.
* ✅ The spine title appears correctly in the exported PDF.
* ✅ All formatting options are applied correctly.
* ✅ The title is centered and fully visible within the spine.
* ✅ No blank spine is shown when a valid title exists.
* ✅ The preview and PDF output are identical.
* ✅ Enabling or disabling the **Book Spine Title Editor** correctly shows or hides the spine title without affecting any other cover elements.
