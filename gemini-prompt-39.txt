In every Kids Facts Book, the section **"PREPARE TO MINI-DISCOVER MAJOR SECRETS!"** consists of **two pages**. Immediately **after these two pages**, insert the following **three additional pages** in the exact order below:

1. **MY FAVORITE FACTS**
2. **FACT EXPLORER CHALLENGE**
3. **AMAZING FACT COLLECTOR**

This structure must be applied consistently to **every current and future book**.

### Index & Page Number Logic

After these three pages are inserted:

* Automatically calculate their actual page numbers during PDF generation.
* Add all three page titles to both the **MAP OF YOUR ADVENTURE** page and the **Alphabetical Subject Index** (where applicable).
* The page numbers shown in the index must always reflect their actual locations in the final PDF.
* If pages are added or removed anywhere in the book, these page numbers must automatically update.

* Render all uploaded SVG icons as high-quality vector graphics.
* Preserve transparency and render the icon artwork as a clean black silhouette.
* Maintain consistent icon size, alignment, spacing, and vertical centering across every entry.
* Do not substitute icons unless the required filename is missing.
* If the required SVG file is not uploaded, leave the icon placeholder empty or display a controlled warning instead of using an incorrect icon.
